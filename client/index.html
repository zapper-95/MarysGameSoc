ALL ARE WELCOME

Everyone is welcome at Game Soc! Whether you've got over a thousand hours on League of Legends or if you've never played Monopoly, we are an inclusive space within the college to discuss and play our favourite games. Look out for more LGBT+ collaborations, as well as collaborations with other societies both at Mary's and at other colleges. In our first year we actively encouraged members of the MCR to participate and are one of the more diverse societies when it comes to age! Membership is free so long as you have paid membership to either the JCR or MCR at Mary's.
